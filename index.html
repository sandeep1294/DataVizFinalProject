Delays Over the last 7 years by each Quarter

The Long Island Rail Road (LIRR) has been the center of debate for many Long Islanders who travel into the city on a daily basis. Outrage over the massive delays in recent years has become more prevalent and widespread that the new president of the MTA has made LIRR improvements a priority.

This graph shows that overall increase in the number of delays over the last seven years. Spikes in the graph show certain times of year where other factors, like weather, affect the delays. However, note that the general trend of the number of delays has increased. While there may have been an increase in the number of riders, the MTA should account for this and give the exception service they promise to give.
